The world of photography and photo manipulation is an exciting hobby that I'm passionate about the topic of photography that I'm interested in is a photography technique called light painting, what is light painting? well, light painting is a long exposure photo taken with a moving light source in either the background or foreground of your subject, the light itself can be used as the subject as well.
